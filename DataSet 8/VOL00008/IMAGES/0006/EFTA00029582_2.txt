UNITED STATES ATTORNEY'S OFFICE

PRESS GUIDANCE
Tuesday, November 23, 2021

*OFF-THE-RECORD, AND FOR GUIDANCE ONLY: Several of the government's anticipated witnesses
will be testifying in this trial using a pseudonym or first name. The Court granted a Government motion
[attached] requesting that they be able to do so, because the victim witnesses are expected to describe alleged
sensitive, explicit and personal events concerning illegal sexual abuse, sexual contact and other acts of which
they have been victims. I believe most, if not all, news media outlets have a policy against identifying sex
crime victims without their consent, and urge you to abide by that policy with our witnesses and respect their
privacy.

Marshall U.S. Courthouse]]).
