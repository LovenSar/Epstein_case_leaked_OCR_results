This letter is respectfully submitted pursuant to United States ex rel. Touhy v.
Regan, 340 U.S. 462 (1951), and the associated regulations, 28 C.F.R. §§ 16.21-16.26.

We are defense counsel of record for Jeffrey Epstein, Reg No. 76318-054, who
was previously incarcerated at the Metropolitan Correctional Center in New York, New
York (the “MCC”) while awaiting trial on federal charges in the above-captioned case.

Attached hereto is a subpoena, issued under Rule 17(c) of the Federal Rules of
Criminal Procedure, seeking any and all medical or mental health records relating to
Mr. Epstein. The requested records are necessary to counsel’s investigation of the
circumstances of Mr. Epstein’s death.

Please provide the requested records by 5:30 p.m., August 26, 2019.

Thank you in advance for your assistance in this matter.
