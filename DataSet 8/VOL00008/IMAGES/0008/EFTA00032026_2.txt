To follow up on our conversation late last week, here is an estimate that covers the scope of work that we discussed.

Here is what I would suggest as a statement of work:

Specific deliverables known at this time include receiving, inventoring and forensically processing approximately 57 devices (a mix of computer hard drives, mobile devices, and portable media); extracting and parsing relevant data from those devices (parsing may involve, for example, review of backups, compressed files or other data that is not initially categorized by forensic tools); creating Relativity load files; preparing summaries and analyses of the data; and briefing the investigative team on the contractor's findings.

As to staffing the engagement, I would propose having two junior-level staff members conduct the review, to be supervised and assisted as necessary by a senior staff member (a former FBI computer scientist). A managing director would have overall responsibility for the project and would coordinate with SDNY.

As to timing: we are available to start work immediately. Estimated time to complete the engagement would be 30 days (or less).

As to a budget, I would estimate between $85,000 to $135,000 as follows:
