A MESSAGE FROM THE EDITOR

The push to deregulate law firm ownership rules is gaining
steam. What makes this time around different? And what
would changes in the rules mean for law firms, ALSPs, the
Big Four, clients and beyond? With access to justice at its
core, these efforts have implications for much more, but
many law firm leaders aren't concerned or don't know what
they'd do with a cash infusion if they got one. Dan Packel
and Dylan Jackson dive into all of these angles, with accompanying pieces from our
global newsroom on what the U.S. can learn from jurisdictions that have
deregulated and what specific policies are being explored in states across the
country.

– Gina Passarella, Editor-in-Chief, The American Lawyer
